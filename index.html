Modern Magic

The rapid and extravagant rise of computing in the modern world has paved the way to new industries, processes and an insurmountable amount of innovation in every corner of the world. Computers act as tools to boost the capabilities of humanity, and have had many positive effects on the environment, society and the scope of achievements that we can reach as a race.

Accuracy is Key

Although the minds of the great pioneers of the pre-modern era were undoubtedly capable of thinking of ideas and concepts in all of their respective sciences, the accuracy that the use of computers enable mean that science can take leaps in more justified, reliable and calculated ways. For instance, we could not have dreamed of going to space without the computational power to calculate and track thousands of variables in realtime, making the miniscule adjustments required for their systems to work.
