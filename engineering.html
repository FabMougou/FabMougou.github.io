ENGINEERING

Is it Possible?

The emergance of computing has meant that engineering can be pushed to the limit of what is possible. Wether it be electrical engineers squeezing all they can out of Moore's Law, or motorsport engineers seeking the peak of vehicular performance in the highest echelon of racing. Computers have contributed in many ways, from creating a more dependable interface to design blueprints and ideas, to simulating the aerodynamics of aeroplanes in variable situations.

Quality and Quantity

Computing has provided a level of abstraction that allows engineers to test their projects without risking them in real world scenarios. The< benefits of this form a lenghty list, but among the most prominent are that it is a much cheaper process, allowing companies to invest in more projects and innovation, and that it is a more thorough process leading to a much safer, higher quality experience for the humans making use of their output.
